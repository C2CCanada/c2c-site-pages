Flagship Asset · Ontario

30 Montcalm, Deep River

Our 32-unit flagship property sits on the Ottawa River in Deep River, Ontario - a stable, purpose-driven community anchored by Canadian Nuclear Laboratories. We acquired it nearly new in 2023, and it has performed exactly the way a well-chosen long-term hold should: consistently occupied, professionally managed, and growing in every meaningful way year over year.

Q4 2025 was the property's strongest quarter of the year. After Q3 included significant planned maintenance - elevator, windows, floors, and fire safety - Q4 returned to normalized expenses with no major or unexpected items. Revenue grew consistently every quarter throughout 2025, and the asset entered 2026 fully occupied, well-reserved, and in excellent condition - and continues to perform accordingly.

This asset is not a development. It's a proven, running operation. The fundamentals that made Deep River attractive when we acquired it - steady demand, low turnover, a community with a clear and stable reason to exist - remain as strong as ever.
