Is This Right for You?

This Is Built for You If:

  • You manage 1–3+ rental properties
  • Your business email runs through Gmail (Google Workspace with your own domain, e.g. you@yourcompany.com)
  • You have regular vendors who send invoices by email
  • You want a system that runs without you managing it
  • You value peace of mind over doing it yourself

This Probably Isn't the Right Fit If:

  • You use Outlook, Yahoo, or a personal @gmail.com address (not Google Workspace)
  • You prefer to customize everything yourself
  • You're still in the first few months of your first property
  • You need ongoing inbox management (a VA role, not an infrastructure install)

Not sure if your email setup qualifies? Google Workspace means your professional email (like you@yourcompany.com) runs through Gmail. If you log into Gmail to check your business email, you're likely on it.
