Our Story

Built by investors who've lived it coast to coast.

Colin Ernst grew up on the East Coast in Halifax, Nova Scotia. Nicole Fiore is from Ontario. They met in Montreal, lived in Vancouver, got married in PEI, and eventually put down roots in Moncton, New Brunswick, exploring and falling in love with this country at every stop.

That journey is more than a name. It shaped how they see real estate: not as a transaction, but as a way to put good people into good homes and build something that lasts.

"We've built our wealth through grit and good decisions. Now we want to protect it and help others do the same."

Since 2017, Coast to Coast Homes Canada has grown from a small portfolio into a multi-province operation spanning Ontario, Nova Scotia, New Brunswick, and British Columbia, managing 150+ doors with the same care and discipline they built from day one.

How We Operate

What you can count on.

We manage the work so you can enjoy the reward. Every investor relationship is built on these principles.

01

Integrity

We do what we say we're going to do, every time. No surprises, no overpromising, no glossing over the hard parts. Honesty is the foundation everything else is built on.

02

Stewardship

We treat your capital like we treat our own. Every asset and investor relationship is managed with diligence, intention, and a long-term view, because that's the only view worth having.
