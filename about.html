About Us

Built on integrity.
Driven by purpose.

Coast to Coast Homes Canada was founded on a simple conviction: that real estate, done right, creates lasting wealth for investors, for communities, and for families. Here's our story.

How It Started

Why "Coast to Coast"?

Colin Ernst is from Halifax, Nova Scotia, East Coast born and raised. Nicole Fiore grew up in Ontario. They met in Montreal, lived in Vancouver, got married in PEI, and eventually settled in Moncton, New Brunswick. Together, they've explored and fallen in love with this country from one end to the other.

That's not just a name. It's the DNA of how they approach real estate. They've seen communities across Canada, understood what people need, and built a portfolio that reflects a genuine belief in this country's potential.

Colin literally drove coast to coast with his best friend years ago. That journey shaped his view that Canada is full of natural beauty, exceptional communities, and real opportunity if you approach it with patience, purpose, and the right partners.

Since 2017, that conviction has grown into a 150-door portfolio spanning Ontario, Nova Scotia, New Brunswick, and British Columbia.

The Journey

Nine years of steady growth.

Not chasing every deal. Building the right ones, in the right markets, with the right people.

2016

The Beginning

Colin and Nicole recognized real estate as a path to financial freedom and long-term wealth. Their drive to build something meaningful, and something that serves others, only strengthened from there.

2017

Coast to Coast Homes Canada Founded

Headquartered in Moncton, New Brunswick. The name reflects their journey and their commitment to building across this country with care and intention.

2019

Portfolio Expansion

Early acquisitions across New Brunswick begin the multi-province strategy. Joint ventures and private lending partnerships bring trusted investors into the fold.

2021

Truro Development Begins

Active development launches in Truro, Nova Scotia: single-family homes, duplexes, and townhomes built to address real housing need in a growing community. Early phases deliver results that set the foundation for what comes next.

2022

Deep River Acquisition

A 32-unit apartment building on the Ottawa River in Deep River, Ontario, the flagship asset, a long-term hold that demonstrates the C2C approach at full scale.

2026

New Phase in Truro. 150+ Doors. 4 Provinces.

A new phase of the Truro development is now opening. The portfolio spans four provinces and continues to grow, always led by integrity, always built for the long game.

How We Show Up

Our core values.

Every investor relationship, every property decision, every communication. These are the six principles that guide how we operate.

01

Integrity

We do what we say we're going to do, every time. Honesty, clarity, and decisions that honor trust. No surprises, no spin, no glossing over the hard parts.

02

Courage

Bold thinking, brave leadership, dreaming beyond limitation. We step into possibility with conviction, because real growth requires the willingness to act before everything is certain.
